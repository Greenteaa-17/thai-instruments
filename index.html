Thai Instrument

Traditional Thai musical instruments (Thai: เครื่องดนตรีไทย, English: Khrueang Dontri Thai) are the musical instruments used in the traditional and classical music of Thailand. They comprise a wide range of wind, string, and percussion instruments played by both the Thai majority as well as the nation's ethnic minorities.

In the traditional Thai system of organology, they are classified into four categories, by the action used in playing:

  • String Instrument
  • Wind Inatrument
  • Percussion Instrument
  • Bowed string instruments
